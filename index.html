About Me

I’m Craig Anderson, a Web Developer based in Toronto. My background in the Film & Television Industry, and my years as a comedian and improv teacher make me an unconventional thinker with a knack for being relentlessly useful.

I take pride in writing efficient and powerful code. I love getting lost the logic of javascript and its possibilities for creating unique and engaging experiences. I’m a builder and a problem solver, and I thrive in collaborative and challenging environments.

When my laptop isn’t warming my legs, I’m usually pouring over baseball stats, building model ships, or teaching improv workshops. I’ve also read too many fantasy novels.
